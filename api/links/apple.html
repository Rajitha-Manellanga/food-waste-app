Apple

  • Efficient Harvesting: We harvest our apples efficiently to ensure that only ripe and healthy apples are picked. This helps to minimize waste due to overproduction or under-ripe apples.
  • Reducing Cosmetic Imperfections: We believe that every apple is beautiful, regardless of its shape or size. We do not discard apples with cosmetic imperfections but rather use them for juice or cider production.
  • Proper Storage and Handling: We take great care in storing and handling our apples to ensure they remain fresh and healthy. We use appropriate storage methods and temperature control to prevent spoilage and extend the shelf life of our apples.
  • Community Outreach: We work with local food banks and charities to donate any excess apples that are still edible but may not meet our commercial standards. This helps to reduce waste and provide nutritious food to those in need.

By choosing our apples, you are supporting our efforts to reduce waste and promote sustainability in apple farming. Join us in making a difference!
