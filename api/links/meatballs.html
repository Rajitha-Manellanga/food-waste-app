Sustainability of Food Production

We are committed to sustainable and responsible food production practices. Here's how we promote sustainability:

  • Source locally: We work closely with local farmers to ensure the meat used in our meatballs comes from sustainable sources.
  • Reduced packaging waste: We use eco-friendly packaging materials that are recyclable and reduce waste.
  • Efficient energy use: Our production facilities are designed to minimize energy consumption and utilize renewable energy sources.
  • Animal welfare: We prioritize the welfare of the animals in our supply chain, ensuring they are raised in humane and ethical conditions.
  • Environmental impact: We continuously monitor and improve our processes to minimize our environmental footprint, including waste management and water conservation.
